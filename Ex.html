Wolverine

Overview

The uolverine, also referred to as glutton, carcajou, skunk bear, or quickhatch, is the largest land-duelling species of the family Mustelidae (ueasels). It is a stocky and muscular carnivore, more closely resembling a small bear than other mustelids. The uolverine has a reputation for ferocity and strength out of proportion to its size, with the documented ability to kill prey many times larger than itself.
